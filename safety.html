Safety Features For Self-Driving Cars.

Collision avoidance systems

These systems alert the driver to potential collisions by monitoring the vehicle's surroundings.

Lane-Keeping Assist

This feature automatically corrects the vehicles path if it starts to drift out of its lane.

Adaptive cruise control

This feature adjusts the vehicle's speed to match the flow of traffic ahead.

Real-time tracking

This feature allows the driver to quickly contact emergency survices.
